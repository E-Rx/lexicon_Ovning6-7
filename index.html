Movie Information

The Count of Monte Cristo

Release Date: August, 2024 (USA)

Director: Alexandre de La Patellière and Matthieu Delaporte

Main Cast: Pierre Niney (Edmond Dantès/Count of Monte Cristo), Bastien Bouillon (Fernand De Morcef), Anaïs Demoustier (Mercedes Herrera), Anamaria Vartolomei (Haydée), Laurent Lafitte (Gérard de Villefort)

Synopsis: The Count of Monte Cristo is an adaptation of Alexandre Dumas' famous novel. The target of a sinister plot, young Edmond Dantes is arrested on his wedding day for a crime he did not commit. After fourteen years in the island prison of Château d'If, he manages a daring escape. Now rich beyond his dreams, he assumes the identity of the Count of Monte Cristo and exacts his revenge on the three men who betrayed him.
